Add value, longevity, and aesthetic appeal to your roof with the team at Seven Roofing. Our expert team specialises in a variety of roofing services including roof restorations, roof repairs, and gutter replacements, providing services for homes and businesses across Melbourne’s eastern and south-eastern suburbs.

No matter what style of property that you have, our fully qualified and insured team of roofers can bring your roof back to its former glory using local Australian products and state-of-the-art equipment.

With our fixed, transparent prices, money-back guarantees, and payment plans available, it’s never been more affordable to restore or repair your roof.

Our Expert Services

  • Roof Restorations

    Our roof restoration services are ideal for those looking to give their roof a new and improved look. It offers many of the benefits of a roof replacement but at a lower cost and in a shorter timeframe.

  • Roof Repairs

    If you’re dealing with leaks, cracking or missing shingles, then our roof repair services are for you. This also includes comprehensive gutter repairs and maintenance work.

  • Roof Replacements

    Whether you’re suffering from a heavily damaged roof or wanting a completely new roof altogether, get in touch with us today for our quality roof replacement services. We’ll make sure your new roof has a stunning look and a long-lasting durable surface.

  • Roof Guttering

    If your roof plumbing has seen better days, you can trust Seven Roofing for all the roof gutter repairs and replacements you need. Gutter maintenance is crucial if you want to make sure your drainage system remains in working condition for years to come.

Everything from Roof Repairs to Gutter Replacement

Every single home is going to have different needs and face different challenges. We always make sure that our roofing and guttering services are specifically tailored to meet your exact requirements.

If you’re not sure what specific service your property needs, we can advise you on whether targeted roof repairs or a complete roof replacement is the best option for you.
